Public Affairs Manager

Amway Vietnam Co., Ltd 21st Fl., Flemington Tower, 182 Le Dai Hanh, Dist.11, HCMC

Apply Now
Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

Job Description

1. Lead & direct the LRBP & AOP public relations strategy & execution to support to Amway business objectives • Lead the development & execution of public affairs & corporate affairs strategy and execution plans to contribute to business planning at the country level • Accountable for budget planning & monitoring effectively 2. Directs and controls the public relation activities in order to enhance the organization's image and reputation with the government,, the media, the employees and the general public • Plan and implement public relations programs, activities, campaigns, events, and develop communication channels that protect and enhance the Amway’s reputation and credibility externally and internally, and that are cost-effective, and appropriate for their intended audience. • Review and approve draft speeches, articles, press releases, public statements, and other communications to ensure that they are accurate and consistent with the Amway overall public relations strategy. • Respond to more complex or escalated enquiries from the public, media, or other interested parties or crisis to ensure that all communications are accurate and appropriate. • Liaise with functional or operational area managers to develop and implement public relations programs or activities that are appropriate for their business needs, but consistent with the Amway overall public relations strategy. 3. Protects, maintains, and enhances the organization’s reputation amongst key government stakeholders and influencers by providing a regular interface and partnering with various government agencies to provide business updates, exchange information promote industry legitimacy and raise public awareness about the industry • Establish and maintain positive relationships at all levels of government, public services, and other local authorities (for example local SoIT, SoH, Market watch, local police etc.) to facilitate communication and help advance the Amway business interests. • Represent the organization’s position on legislation and government policy to government representatives, committees, and other regulatory agencies so that the Amway interests are advanced. • Monitor, analyze, and interpret developments in government policy and advise senior management on how these trends may impact the Amway business interests as well as ensure all business activities are aligned with local laws & regulations • Manage the organization’s most high level, complex, controversial, or critical government relationships. 4. Partner with government agencies or organizations and MLM committee to enhance Amway’s credibility and reputation • Initiate, implement & publicize sustainable CSR projects & sponsorship programs • Engage with VMLMA and AMCHAM as Member 5. Lead, direct, evaluate, and develop a qualified & high engaged team of Public Affairs to achieve established PA goals. • Acquire & recruit qualified staff at right position • Strengthen staff’s skillful workforce through consistent on the job training programs as well as the appropriate job-related external trainings courses. • Manage staff’s performance, motivate and reward effectively & efficiently for staff’s high engagement • Plan & manage career path planning & talent development for staff retention & meeting business job scopes expanded requirement • Maximize resources effectively 6. Lead important regional initiatives and others ad-hoc projects as required

Job Requirement

1. BA degree or MBA graduation, specializing in Mass Communication and Public Relation are preferable 2. Thorough knowledge and experiences in corporate affairs & public relation management 3. Strong competency and experience in press and corporate write up 4. At least 3 year experience in PA Manager position 5. Strong vision & strategic thinking ability and strategic business planning skills 6. High adaptability & change management abilities 7. Excellent in communication & presentation skills in English and Vietnamese 8. Good interpersonal & partnership building skills 9. Strong leadership & managerial skills 10. Positive attitude & high level of maturity 11. Willing to travel frequently

Amway – Where life gets better Amway Vietnam is an affiliate of Amway Corporation, the world’s leader in direct selling with more than 3 million Independent Business Owners and 20,000 employees in over 100 countries and territories. Founded in 1959 with headquarters in Ada, Michigan, U.S., Amway has grown to become one of the world’s largest direct selling businesses powered by well-known brands and unique opportunities. with the annual sales exceeding USD 11.3 billion in 2012. Amway Vietnam launched its business as 100% foreign-owned company in April 2008. We have our own manufacturing facility in Amata Industrial Zone, Dong Nai, producing quality products in nutrition, beauty and homecare. We also have branches and regional distribution centers in HCMC, Hanoi, Hai Phong, Da Nang, Can Tho, Vinh, Nha Trang, Bac Giang and Ca Mau. Since the beginning, we have focused on laying a solid foundation for the development of our business through the training of our distributors and staff, compliance of our rules of conduct, business ethics, laws and regulations. Our vision is to work each and every day to help people live better lives. We achieve our vision by helping people discover their potential and achieve their goals by offering better products and opportunities, and by sharing with the community. For more information, please visit our website: www.amway2u.com. We are looking for high-caliber candidates to join our dynamic team and support the expansion of our business.
