Samsung Electro-Mechanics(www.samsungsem.co.kr) is one of the world's largest parts and components manufacturer that produces state-of-the-art electronic and mechanical components. Since its foundation in 1973, the company continues to develop and produce new next generation products based on its core electronic parts such as chip, PCB, ISM, RF and Power products. Other than the IT business, the company is also focusing its management capacity on future businesses such as EV, Vehicle, Energy and Bio which will be promising industries in the future. The company has 11 production sites and numerous sales offices worldwide. Plants in Korea are focusing mainly on R&D and core parts manufacturing, China has multi production facilities, Thailand has a RF Facility, and the Philippines has a chip parts facility. The company emphasizes global business with the consideration of local culture and specialized local services and now the company is expanding its business and digital technology service by establishing a new corporation in Vietnam. Samsung Electro-Mechanics will establish a Vietnamese Corporation (SEMV) in Yen Binh Industrial Park, Thai Nguyen of Vietnam in 2014. The factory site will be about 265,000 ㎡ and HDI, ISM and adaptor products will be mainly manufactured. Therefore, we would like to recruit excellent experienced employees in the fields of public affair, environmental safety, finance, and education. Also, we will provide many opportunities for further abroad education and training and personal trainings for individual growth to career employees. If you are interested, please join us.
