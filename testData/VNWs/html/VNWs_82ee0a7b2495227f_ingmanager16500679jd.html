Sales & Marketing Manager

Fujifilm Vietnam Co.,ltd P.2001, 20th Floor, Gemadept Tower, 2Bis-4-6 Le Thanh Ton, Ben Nghe Ward, Dist 1, HCMC

Apply Now

Job Description

A. POSITION SUMMARY: 1. Responsible for developing new business and product range. 2. Be a key member of the company senior management team to successfully implement the plan of the new business identified. B. MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES: 1. Research and evaluate customers, products, markets, competitors; analyze and contribute to marketing and sales (order plan, market share, pricings) strategies together with suppliers; negotiate with supplier for support’s activities to deploy plans in Vietnam in order to ensure supplier’s targets set reasonably market and company resources 2. Formulate marketing plan, lead and direct marketing activities, allocate works and monitor implementation activities; in order to develop brand awareness 3. Formulate sales plan (product distribution, profit margin, target market segment, market coverage, manpower, bounded warehouse), develop pricing and credit policies; oversee sales activities; deal with key accounts on pricing and delivery terms; approve calculation sheet; support order administrations process; monitor and follow up A/R collection; in order to achieve sales and profit margin targets 4. Identify and develop new businesses to expand the company product range and customer base. 2. Make business plan and organize resources to fulfil orders secured as mentioned in item 1. 3. Prepare monthly report on progress of all new enquiries and projects. 4. Develop work’s process, procedures and standard policies of business line; monitor implementation plans in order to improve department performance 5. Hire, coach and make career development plan for sales engineers; work allocation and manage team performance in order to improve staff’s competencies and efficiency.

Job Requirement

* A tertiary education, preferably in Marketing, Sales or Engineering • At least 3 year experience in the same position (SALE MANAGER) • Fluent English • Possess a proven and good track record in working with people • Possess good business acumen with experience in marketing and/or sales • Work experience in an Electrical/Electronic field will be advantageous ***Only the selected candidates will be contacted for an interview.
