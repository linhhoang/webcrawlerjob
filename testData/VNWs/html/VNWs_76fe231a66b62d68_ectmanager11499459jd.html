Job Description

RAKUS CO., LTD is the provider of original cloud services. We set our each cloud product as a project. A project is divided into some teams. 1. Make arrangements about product specification, specification changes and new functional additions with the Planning Development members of RAKUS Japan. 2. Help the team leaders and programmers understand the contents correctly about product specification, specification changes, or new functional additions. 3. Create the master schedule and WBS in order to ensure the delivery time of a product strictly. 4. Progress Management Based on WBS 5. PM will prepare and enforce the backup plan in case of Development Delay 6. Report (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, etc.) to the Planning and Development member of RAKUS Japan. 7. Manage, evaluate, and give personnel training for your team leaders. *** BENEFIT 1. Training in Japan - You have 3 months training to learn "professional & advanced application development" in Tokyo/Osaka, Japan. - You will be able to "build a decent environment for web service with big business size", "be experienced with advanced cloud server and version control system", "learn anti-hacking security with using security test system". - You study these knowledge in the first half of the training. You join to Japanese development team and acquire more practical skills through the actual projects in the second half. - We prepare the highest traveling insurance and Visa for you. - We supply decent and private accommodation. - Daily working transportation fee is also paid by us. - In addition to others, we provide you $30 expense per day. 2. Salary 1,000 USD – 1,400 USD 3. Awards of excellent employees and groups, conferment of prizes, overseas training institution etc. 4. Many promotion chances to higher positions. (for example : Step up to General Manager of Development Dept.) 5. Working environment: dynamic, innovative and friendly. 6. Subscription to foreign company's health insurance, annual company trip, team building activities

Job Requirement

- Be able to manage the members of a team as a team leader and lead to the success of the team by self-development - Have development experiences of the Web application by JAVA or PHP as a team leader. (Experience of Project Manager is not required.） - Have the capability of the technical communication in Japanese with Japanese IT engineers. (The qualification of Japanese capability, such as Nihongo nouryoku Kentei is not required.） (JapanWorks)
