Job Description

Being a Team Leader/Scrum master you will have responsibilities: 1. Work quality and planning: - Produce project estimates of works, resources and time - Analyze project requirements - Ensure quality of projects and solution architecture - Communicate, support customers in regard to project products - Divide project work among team members - Assist team members to resolve technical issues..etc 2. Teams Management: - Assess performance, ability and attributes of all team members and co-workers participating in and / or assisting project execution, monthly and at the end of project phases - Encourage team to record their timesheets and update their work progress. Control and report this activity More detail will be provide in the interview.

Job Requirement

MUST have all in the following : - Bachelor or higher education in Computer sciences, IT Software - Good Experience in MS technologies: C#/VB, ASP.NET, Windows Communication Foundation, Windows Presentation Foundation, .NET Framework 2.0/3.0/ 3.5/4.0/4.5, MS SQL Server 2005/2008/2012/2014 - Advantages with Microsoft certifications such as MCTS and/or MCPD - At least 5 years working in software field, 1 year team management (team from 5 members) - Good communication, personal, management, negotiation and teamwork skills - Very good English skill (Speaking, writing, listening, reading) NICE TO HAVE; - Good in understanding customers’ needs. - Very good capability builder. - Can engage directly with end customers - Experiences /or having been working with scrum / agile development - Microsoft Exchange Server 2007/2010/2013/Exchange Online. - TOEIC 785 is a plus. WHAT WE OFFER YOU: - Competitive Salary - Good working environment - Exciting leisure: sport, tea break, the company teamwork quarterly - Company’s labor policy completely pursuant to Vietnamese labor legislation (social & health insurance). Plus other benefit offer by the company (annual leave extend, personal healthcare-Bảo Việt care)
