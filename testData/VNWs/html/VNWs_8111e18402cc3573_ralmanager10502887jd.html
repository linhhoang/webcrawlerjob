Job Description

• To manage an overall business activities through General Director and BOD ( Board of Director ) • To manage and maintain the production to achieve of quality goals, productivity, cost controllable, employee and safety environment through the law and local community as concern. • To participate in the business and strategic plans and budgets. • To keep an updated on competitive and market shared and trends, and/or other factors which may have an impact of business. • To develop new business and new customers to grow a company. • To drive sale and factory departments to achieve company target or KPIs.
