Established in 1999, YKVN is a top Vietnam based law firm with a diversified national and international practice. With more than sixty lawyers in offices in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, we recently established an office in Singapore to continue the expansion of our cross-border practice. YKVN is a market leader in M&A, corporate finance, project finance, banking, foreign investment, litigation and arbitration, international trade and tax. With the Singapore expansion, we will be targeting growth in cross-border M&A, inbound and outbound, real estate, fund formation, international arbitration, cross-border capital markets, project finance and shipping. In 2014, YKVN won for the eighth time the prestigious International Financial Law Review’s “Vietnam Law Firm of the Year” award. YKVN has won the award for eight out of the ten years that it has been awarded to Vietnamese law firms, including the original award in 2004.
