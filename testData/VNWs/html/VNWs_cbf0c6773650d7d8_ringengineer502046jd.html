Mechanical Manufacturing Engineer

GE Power & Water Hai Phong Nomura Haiphong Industrial Zone - An Duong, Hai Phong

Apply Now
Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

Job Description

Job Summary: To be a Manufacturing Engineer to support manufacturing of the O&G Valve and Fabricaton product in Hai Phong factory. in this role, you will be familiar to API system and other standard such as ASME, API, ASTM, EN, BS etc, and to provide engineering expertise during manufacturing operation including development, optimization the manufacturing process, so as to achieve: best quality, less labor time, quicker lead time for our products Essential Functions / Responsibilities: - Provide technical expertises / solution for daily manufacturing technical issues - Be responsible for the manufacturing process development including BOM, MPP, and WI. - Prepare tool & fixture & equipment for production - Manufacturing cost estimation, sourcing Technical support, Quality qualifications support, product cost reductions efforts, and NPI projects implementation - Perform Six Sigma and Lean manufacturing projects - Provide technical leadership for manufacturing and testing processes and quality improvement of products - Own or support manufacturing engineering projects requiring development of specifications, purchase, installation and qualification of manufacturing equipment - Participate in EHS related actions to improve plant safety in all areas

Job Requirement

Qualifications / Requirements: - Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering or Engineering Technology from an accredited college or university with Mechanical or Welding Engineer Background - Have at least three years working experience in fabrication manufacturing job - Have experience with NDT & welding inspection - Can read Technical code/standard and technical document both in Vietnamese & English - Experienced with API, ASTM, BS, ASME standards Desired Characteristics: - Prior experience with ship building, fabrication & welding job - Tool or process design experience - Six Sigma Green Belt certified is prefered - Prior experience with Lean manufacturing is prefered - Open & Honest and Trustworthly - Can work invidually and/or in a team and can handle high working pressure / Resilience - Up to date technical Knowledge of the latest trends in manufacturing Technology and ability to apply and adapt - Team work & leadership skills
