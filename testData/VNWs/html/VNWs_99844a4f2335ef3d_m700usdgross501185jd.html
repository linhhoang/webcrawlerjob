Job Description

* ONLY ACCEPT JAPANESE SPEAKERS AND QUALIFIED CANDIDATES * Probation period: 2 months Probation period will be start from October or November * Contract period: 1 year * Working conditions: - Working days: Monday to Friday - Working time: 08:00 to 17:00 - Lunch break: 12:00 to 13:00 * Report to: General Manager/ the person appointed by the General Manager of the Representative Office. * Main duties: - Assist the Representative Office in the conducting administrative tasks for the purpose of communicating and facilitating the implementation of contracts and agreements entered into between NIPPO CORPORATION and Vietnamese parties as directed / instructed by the General Manager or other person in charge of its work from time to time ; and - Other works instructed by the General Manager in accordance with Vietnamese law. - Interpret from Vietnamese, Japanese and vice verse. - Translate document from Vietnamese, English, Japanese and vice verse. - Assess our Personal Income Tax (Monthly, Final return) - Keep financial reports. - Make payment and cash withdrawal work at Bank. - Provide general secretarial and administrative tasks for office

Job Requirement

- Female - Below 35 years old - University degree in business management or related majors. - Working experience at Japanese company for more than 2 years. - Good communication skills (writing and speaking) in Japanese (more than N2 level) and English (more than TOEIC 700 worth). - Possess strong EXCEL and WORD skills. - Good work ethics and capacity to learn and adapt. - Be able to adapt quickly to changing priorities, able to handle multiple tasks simultaneously - Responsible, effective problem-solving, critical thinking and dedicated - Flexible to multitasks, hardworking and friendly * Benefit: - Salary range: From USD 700(Gross) - Insurance: Health Insurance, Social Insurance - Paid Holiday: 12 days per year - Bonus: 1 month salary per year on every January.
