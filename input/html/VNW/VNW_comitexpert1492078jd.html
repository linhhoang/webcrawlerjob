IT Expert

Vietnamworks Client
District 1, Ho Chi Minh City

Apply Now
Save job | Add job alert for similar jobs

Job Description

1. To be responsible of the proper functioning of the company’s IT Infrastructure, including VoIP PBX Server and office equipment (Fax, scanner, printers, photocopiers, television, projectors, etc.);
2. To be in charge of planning the development of the IT infrastructure, providing the solutions for all IT needs of the company;
3. Develop policies and standard operating procedures to ensure smooth running of IT network and systems, including LAN network, servers, Active Directory, databases, anti-virus, print services, telephone, fax and email communication;
4. Proactively monitor system resources including server storage space, Internet bandwidth, virus/spyware/malware activities, server/device logs;
5. Support users with IT-related issues in a responsive and service oriented manner;
6. Provide clean, clear and precise IT documentation that is appropriate for both technical and non-technical audience.

Job Requirement

REQUIREMENTS
1. Network administration experience required;
2. Experience with wire/wireless networking and security;
3. Expert in network design and system administration (Firewall, Routers, Microsoft Windows Servers, Linux servers, VLAN, Backup, Antivirus, Mail service, etc.);
4. Expert in user support, including Windows Vista/7/8, Macintosh 10.x, Office Suite for Windows and Mac, PDF conversion and image/QR code processing, and mobile devices (iOS, Android, Blackberry, Windows Phone);
5. Good knowledge and/or experience on Open-source VoIP PBX System (Asterisk/FreeSwitch);
6. Experience in virtualization (Microsoft Hyper-V) is a plus.

OTHER REQUIREMENTS AND SOFT SKILLS
1. High sense of confidentiality and responsibility;
2. Effective oral, electronic and written communication in both English and Vietnamese;
3. Disciplined, thorough, hard working, responsible, trustworthy, honest, committed;
4. Excellent interpersonal, team building;
5. Demonstrated prioritization and organizational skills
6. Excellent time management skills and ability to prioritize competing requests;
7. Excellent problem solving and analytical skills with strong attention to details;
8. Demonstrated ability to handle private and confidential information;
9. Ability to learn department functions and new technology quickly;
10. Excellent training skill;
11. Ability to work independently and manage multiple timelines while maintaining the team focus;
12. Can consistently follow regulations and instructions;
13. Passionate, optimistic, and has internal inspiration to get things done.

REPORTING LINE
1. The IT Expert will report directly to the Chief Representative;
2. For any new installation on user’s equipment, the IT Expert needs to seek for the approval of the Chief Representative in advance;
3. Any issue or problem if occur needs to be reported immediately to the Chief Representative.
