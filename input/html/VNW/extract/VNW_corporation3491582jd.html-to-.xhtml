Apply Now

Job Description

• Being IT Support Engineer for a well-known leading global IT corporation.
• Working with medium and big size organizations, helping them to solve ongoing issues with different software solutions.
• Respond, perform troubleshooting analysis requests for technical assistance via phone, email, virtual sessions and any other form of communication in a timely, effective, and efficient manner.
• Diagnose and resolve technical software issues.
• Identify and escalate situations requiring urgent attention, understanding business impact and priorities.
• Document, track, and report product system issues and customer enhancement requests.
• Language of communication: all in English
• Reporting to Team leader

Job Requirement

• Graduate from technical schools, private school or have Diploma in IT related discipline.
• At least two years of IT system experience.
• Very good to excellent English skills. Customer oriented
• A team player with good interpersonal skills as well as ability to work independently.
• Highly proactive person. Eager to learn. Logical thinking,
• Ready to work at evening shift, weekends and holidays when it is necessary.

Technical skills desired – in order of importance:
- OS/Sys admin: Windows/Windows Server/Unix/Linux system management
- Database admin/Scripting – MSSQL/Oracle; SQL; Java/Perl/XML/C…
- Web and application – IIS/Apache; Exchange/Lotus Notes; Web Logic; Web Sphere…
- Networking – Intermediate to expert knowledge
- Storage & NAS

We offer
• Highly competitive salary and performance bonus
• Training and certification
• Career path opportunities
• Private healthcare and accident insurance together with compulsory insurance
• Friendly and fun work environment

For immediate attention, send your resume (two pages maximum) to Ms. Phuong Hong. All applications will be confidential
